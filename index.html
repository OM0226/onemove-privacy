Introduction

Welcome to ONE MOVE. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ONE MOVE (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect minimal information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Username: An optional display name you choose for your profile
  • Customization Choices: Your selected profile icons, themes, and titles
  • Game Progress: Puzzles completed, achievement levels, and scores

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: Game session statistics and feature usage
  • Performance Data: Crash reports (if crashes occur)

1.3 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage:

  • Game progress and statistics
  • Gem earnings and spending history
  • Achievement level progress
  • Customization purchases
  • Daily Challenge completion status
  • User preferences and settings

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide core gameplay functionality
  • Track your progress and achievements
  • Save your game state and preferences
  • Improve the App and fix technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

3. Data Sharing

3.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3.2 Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Apple Analytics) to help us understand how the App is used. These services have their own privacy policies.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Secure local storage on your device
  • Regular security updates

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains until you:

  • Delete the App
  • Clear browser/app data
  • Use any reset features

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing

CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS: You have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what information is collected and the right to opt-out of data sales. We do not sell personal information.

EUROPEAN RESIDENTS: You have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability.

To exercise your rights, contact us at onemovegame@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

ONE MOVE is designed for users of all ages, including children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require children to provide personal information to use the App
  • The App is safe for children - no ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection

If you believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us at onemovegame@gmail.com

8. Third-Party Services

8.1 Analytics

We may use analytics services like Google Analytics or Apple App Analytics. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

8.2 Apple Game Center (If Applicable)

If we integrate Apple Game Center:

  • Your Game Center username may be visible to other players
  • Achievement data may be synced with Game Center
  • Apple's Privacy Policy applies to Game Center features

9. No Advertising

ONE MOVE does not display third-party advertisements. We do not share your information with advertising networks.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App indicates acceptance of changes.
