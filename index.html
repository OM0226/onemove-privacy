1. Introduction

Welcome to ONE MOVE! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our puzzle game application.

2. Information We Collect

ONE MOVE is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Local Storage Only

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Game Progress: Your current level, score, and puzzle completion status
  • Premium Status: Whether you have unlocked the full game
  • Daily Challenge: Daily puzzle completion status and streak count
  • Customization: Your selected profile icon, theme, and title preferences
  • Settings: Sound preferences and other game settings

This data never leaves your device and is stored using your device's local storage mechanisms.

3. Free Trial and Premium Unlock

ONE MOVE offers a free trial followed by an optional one-time purchase to unlock the full game.

How It Works

  • Free Trial: The first 30 puzzles are completely free to play
  • Premium Unlock: After completing 30 puzzles, you can unlock the full game for a one-time payment of $4.99 USD
  • Full Game Access: Premium unlock gives you unlimited access to all current and future puzzles

Purchase Information

  • Payment Processing: All purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store through your Apple ID
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to us
  • Payment Information: We never see or store your credit card, payment method, or billing information
  • Receipt Validation: Apple provides us with receipt data to verify legitimate purchases. This verification happens locally on your device
  • Premium Status: Your premium unlock status is stored locally on your device only

Refunds

All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. For refund requests, please contact Apple Support directly through your App Store account or at reportaproblem.apple.com.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data in the first place. Your gameplay data and premium status remain entirely on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

ONE MOVE does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else. The game can be safely enjoyed by users of all ages without privacy concerns. Parents should be aware that in-app purchases are available and should use Apple's parental controls if desired.

6. Analytics and Tracking

We do NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking cookies
  • Third-party SDKs that collect data
  • User behavior tracking

7. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists as long as you keep the app installed
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local game data and premium status
  • You can clear your data at any time through your device's settings
  • Important: If you uninstall the app, you will need to restore your premium purchase through Apple's App Store using "Restore Purchases"

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible to you at any time
  • Deletion: Simply uninstall the app or clear app data through device settings
  • Portability: Your data is not portable as it exists only on your device
  • Purchase History: View your purchase history through your Apple ID account

9. Security

Your data security is inherent in our design:

  • No personal data is transmitted over the internet
  • No servers store your information
  • All data remains under your device's security protections
  • Payment security is handled by Apple's secure infrastructure

10. Apple App Store

When you make a purchase, you are subject to Apple's privacy policy and terms of service. Apple may collect information about your purchase, including:

  • Your Apple ID
  • Purchase date and amount
  • Payment method information
  • Device information

Please review Apple's Privacy Policy for more information about how Apple handles your data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. California Privacy Rights

Under California law (CCPA), California residents have the right to request information about data collection and sharing. Since we don't collect or share any personal information, there is no data to request, delete, or opt-out from.

13. International Users

ONE MOVE can be used worldwide. Since we don't collect any personal data, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. All purchases are processed according to Apple's regional pricing and policies.
